Than it is to appreciate.. but that would be missing the point.. there is always scope for improvement.. but that's not the point.. the point is to appreciate the beauty - and you can always find it if you look hard enough - of the current state. To enjoy this beauty. Then, you can go about improving it.. not because you don't you don't like the current state.. but because you love what you see in it and want to make it so much better.
