I want to become better at evaluating and consolidating the information I consume. And big goals require [effective processes](http://ilearnthereforeiam.blogspot.sg/2013/12/working-with-processes-and-goals.html) to support them 

  

Hence I have started a series of blog posts. The rules are:

1.  Post length is limited to 120 words (to make me think and consolidate),
2.  Every post expresses 1 idea / view point.
3.  3 times a week to create a habit.

Let's see how this goes!
