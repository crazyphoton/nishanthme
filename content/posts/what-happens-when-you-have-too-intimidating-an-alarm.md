date: 2009-09-10T08:38:00.001+08:00
draft: false
url: /2009/09/what-happens-when-you-have-too.html
---

You are so focused on switching off and going back to sleep - so, though it serves the purpose of waking you up, it does not help you stay awake.  
  
It might be a good idea to follow it up always with a softer one which will remind you that the objective is to keep awake and not switch the annoying alarm off :)
