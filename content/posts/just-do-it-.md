And give it your best! Your BEST.. when you do something to high levels of perfection, the satisfaction and the control you feel are great.. As you know something better, as you know it very deeply.. its gives you a sense of completeness. Something to really appreciate. It is something everyone should strive towards..

  

Not so you become the best in the world at something, but simply to understand something completely.  

  

And practice is the only thing that makes perfect. :)
